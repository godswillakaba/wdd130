Adventure Tips & Safety

  • Wear safety Gear: Always wear a helmet and a life jacket
  • Stay hydrated: Bring a reuseable water bottle
  • Pack light: Water shoes, suncreen, and extra clothes are a must
  • Follow the Guide: Listen to your guide's instruction at all times
  • Weather check: Ensure the conditions are save for rafting
